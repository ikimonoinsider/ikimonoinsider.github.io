Join the Clique!

In order to join the clique, send an e-mail to hello@creaturesinsi.de with the following:

- Your Name
- Your Icon (please crop it to 100x100px!)
- Creature from Shelter (name and palette, or a picture of it)
- Creature's Name
- Website Link

Give us a few days to check the forms. You will get a reply if you've been accepted!

Guidelines

- After you're approved, put your Creature on your website with a link back to Creatures Inside -- No hotlinking!

- If you change URLs or delete website, please let us known, or else you will permanently lose your Creature and it will be put up for adoption

- Sites that host bigoted content or are solely focused on NSFW content are not allowed. A NSFW section is fine though
